ChronTools
An online tool for players of Chronicles of Darkness
Chronicles of Darkness is a tabletop role-playing game set in the modern world with one twist: the supernatural are real. There are many types of game modes that use the Chronicles of Darkness system:
Vampire: The Requiem
A game of personal horror, wherein players play vampires.
Werewolf: The Forsaken
A game of savage fury, wherein players take on the role of werewolves.
Mage: The Awakening
A game of modern sorcery, wherein players take on the role of mages.
Promethean: The Created
A game of stolen lives, wherein players take the role of Prometheans.
Changeling: The Lost
A game of beautiful madness in which players take the role of Changelings.
Hunter: The Vigil
A game of light and shadows, in which players take the role of hunters.
Geist: The Sin-Eaters
A game of second chances, in which players take the role of Sin-Eaters.
Mummy: The Curse
A game of immortal souls, where players play the roles of the Arisen.
