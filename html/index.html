Welcome to Chronicles of Darkness
Chronicles of Darkness is a series of rule books by White Wolf Publishing which describe a world where the supernatural have hidden in plain sight within human society throughout all of history. This world supports a multitude of roles for you and your friends to play, from a simple human hunting down the various supernatural that plague the Earth to a Vampire Marquis trying to surge to the top of his order. These books combine to form a beautiful canvas painting a dark and grim world, ready to be molded by your hands.
