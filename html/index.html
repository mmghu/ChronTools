ChronTools provides many useful tools and trackers for players of Chronicles of Darkness, both in and out of session.
Uses In Session:
Track useful data like health, willpower, and beats
Roll dice with specific CoD functionality like 9 again and 10 again
Use quick reference materials for combat, social maneuvering, conditions, and more
Keep notes and observations from the session and share with team mates
Uses Out of Session:
Store and edit your character sheet online
Chat with friends in character during downtime
Manage and edit your CoD groups
